Interview With Heidi Hayes

What is Relay?

Relay for Life at Colonel By Secondary School is an annual event that raises funds and awareness towards research and caring for those affected by the disease.
For twelve hours on June 1st 2018, students will come together out on the field with their teammates supporting and celebrating the progress of eliminting cancer forever.

What happens at relay?

  • A festival-like fundraising event where team members take turns walking around the track
  • Teams bring their own tents, coolers, snacks, etc
  • Activities and live performances through-out the entire day

History of Relay

With an extensive and impressive history at Colonel By, it's easy to see why Relay For Life is the year's most anticipated event

  • $800K raised so far

  • #1 High School in Ottawa

  • $100K Annualy

  • Top 4 in Ontario
